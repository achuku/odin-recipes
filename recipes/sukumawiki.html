Sukuma Wiki Recipe

Description

Sukuma wiki(collard greens) is a vegetable dish

It is consumed widely and mostly used as an accompaniment to a starch. It is a staple in Kenya

Ingredients

  • Sukuma wiki leaves
  • 2 onions
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut your sukuma wiki leaves into small edible pieces
  2. Cut the onions into small pieces
  3. Heat some cooking oil in a pot and cook the onions in low heat until they turn brownish
  4. Add your cut sukuma wiki and simmer for 10 minutes
