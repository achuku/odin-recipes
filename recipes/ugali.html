Ugali Recipe

Description

Ugali is a cake looking dish made from maize flour.

It is consumed in many parts of Africa and goes by a variety of names depending on the level of softness.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place some water in a cooking pot and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add maize flour in small portions as you stir.
  3. Knead the mixture using a flat based cooking stick under moderate heat.
  4. Keep kneading the mixture as it hardens, making sure to flatten the forming lumps.
  5. The meal should be ready after about 8 to 10 minutes of repeating the last 2 steps.
  6. Serve on a plate and consume with your favorite stew.
